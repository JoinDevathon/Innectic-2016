Theme is Machines.

Idea is farming.

How is works:

Place a diamond, iron, or gold block in a field of crops.

Diamond:
    Harvests 4 x 4 x 1

Gold:
    Harvests 2 x 2 x 1

Iron:
    Harvests: 1 x 1 x 1

Config structure:
    farmingBlocks:
        1:
            location:
                x: 1
                y: 2
                z: 3
            type: diamond
