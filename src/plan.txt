Theme is Machines.

Idea is farming.

How is works:

Place a diamond, iron, or gold block in a field of crops.

Diamond:
    Instagrow
Gold:
    Half the normal time
Iron:
    1/4 of the time

Config structure:
    farmingBlocks:
        lastID: 1
        1:
            location:
                x: 1
                y: 2
                z: 3
            type: diamond
